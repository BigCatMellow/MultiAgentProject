## THE SHOPKEEPER FIGURE — NAME NOT YET PINNED

[OPEN — IMPORTANT] Across drafts and sessions this character has been called the **Shopkeeper**, the **Bookkeeper**, and the **Keeper**, and "the ledger/Bookkeeper" is also used for the *concept* of where prunes are recorded. This is a live inconsistency and a likely source of confusion.

Two distinct things may need two distinct names:
1. The **person/entity** who runs the Space Between and charges for transactions.
2. The **ledger function** — where a ledgered prune is recorded.

Decision needed: pick one name for the person, decide whether the ledger is the same entity or a separate concept, and pin spelling. Until then, do not treat any single spelling as canon. Tracked in the conflicts doc.

## NAMING LOGIC (for consistency when new names are needed)

- Human-world surnames as names for the old practitioners (Pathwell, Stansbury) — plain, slightly antique, not fantasy-exotic.
- Camp names lean warm/folk (Mama Baga, Boots).
- The extraordinary is named plainly, not portentously — consistent with the dry tone. Avoid names that announce their own significance. (This is exactly the tension flagged around "Shade.")
