Rating definitions:

- `PRIMARY` — authoritative, first-party, or canonical for the claim.
- `SECONDARY` — reputable but interpreted or aggregated.
- `COMMUNITY` — unverified but plausible (forum, blog, issue thread).
- `UNVERIFIED` — unknown provenance, or model recall with no live source
  check.
- `STALE` — was authoritative but is date-sensitive and may be outdated.
